AI Study Buddy - UX Design Directions

These design directions explore different visual and interaction approaches for the AI Study Buddy, building upon our chosen color palette and design system (shadcn/ui).

1. Minimalist & Focused

A clean, spacious approach emphasizing content and direct calls to action, minimizing distractions for efficient learning.

  • Layout: Clean, spacious, single-column focus for content.
  • Visual Hierarchy: Subtle headers, strong emphasis on core content.
  • Interaction: Direct, clear calls to action, minimal distractions.
  • Visual Weight: Light, abundant white space.
  • Content Approach: Text-focused, highly scannable.
  • Personality: Professional & Efficient.
  • Best for: Users who want to get straight to the point, minimal cognitive load.

2. Card-Based & Engaging

Organizes documents, summaries, and quizzes into visually distinct cards, promoting an interactive and approachable browsing experience.

  • Layout: Grid of cards for documents, summaries, quizzes.
  • Visual Hierarchy: Clear card titles, visual cues for status and type.
  • Interaction: Clickable cards for details, intuitive navigation.
  • Visual Weight: Balanced, clear structure with defined boundaries.
  • Content Approach: Visually rich (e.g., document thumbnails on cards), scannable.
  • Personality: Friendly & Approachable.
  • Best for: Users who prefer visual organization and a more interactive browsing experience.
(Full-screen mockups of key screens would be displayed here in a comprehensive showcase.)

3. Guided Workflow & Progressive

A step-by-step interface for key actions like uploading and generating content, providing clear guidance and progress indication.

  • Layout: Step-by-step interface for key actions (upload, generate).
  • Visual Hierarchy: Prominent current step indicator, clear progress.
  • Interaction: Wizard-like flow, clear next/back actions, focused on one task at a time.
  • Visual Weight: Balanced, guiding the user through a process.
  • Content Approach: Focused on one task at a time, with clear instructions.
  • Personality: Supportive & Easy.
  • Best for: Users who need more hand-holding, especially for complex tasks.
