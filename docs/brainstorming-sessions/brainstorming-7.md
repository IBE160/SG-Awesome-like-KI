## Success Metrics

### Immediate Opportunities
- Personalized quiz "workout plans" (daily vs. bootcamp)
  - At least 50% of all quizzes taken utilize either the 'daily' or 'bootcamp' mode.
- Targeted feedback on weak chapters
  - Students who receive targeted feedback on a weak chapter are 40% more likely to attempt another quiz on that same chapter within 24 hours.
- Progress charts
  - Users who view their progress charts are 20% more likely to complete a quiz session that day.

### Future Innovations
- Achievements, trophies, or grades
  - An increase in user engagement (e.g., quizzes completed per week) by users who actively pursue achievements.
- Friendly competitions
  - At least 15% of active users participate in a friendly competition each month.

### Moonshots
- AI-powered forgetting curve prediction and proactive refreshers
  - A measurable increase in long-term retention of learned material, as evidenced by performance on follow-up assessments.
