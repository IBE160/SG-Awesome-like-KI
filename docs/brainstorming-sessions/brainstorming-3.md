### Immediate Opportunities
- **Personalized quiz "workout plans" (daily vs. bootcamp)**
  - *Success Metric:* At least 50% of all quizzes taken utilize either the 'daily' or 'bootcamp' mode.
- **Targeted feedback on weak chapters**
  - *Success Metric:* Students who receive targeted feedback on a weak chapter are 40% more likely to attempt another quiz on that same chapter within 24 hours.
- **Progress charts**
  - *Success Metric:* Users who view their progress charts are 20% more likely to complete a quiz session that day.

### Future Innovations
- **Achievements, trophies, or grades** (Note: These are app-specific mastery levels, not official academic grades)
  - *Success Metric:* An increase in user engagement (e.g., quizzes completed per week) by users who actively pursue achievements.
- **Friendly competitions**
  - *Success Metric:* At least 15% of active users participate in a friendly competition each month.
